Questions

When will I get my items?

The shipping estimates above tell you when your items are expected to arrive. As each item leaves our warehouse, we’ll email you with delivery and tracking information.
Ordered more than one item? You’ll get a separate email as each item ships. There are no additional shipping fees for these items.

How do I view or change my order?

Visit online Order Status to view the most up-to-date status and make changes to your order. To learn more about shipping, changing your order, or returns, please visit online Help.
You can also call Apple Support at 800-692-7753 Monday through Friday from 9:00 a.m. to 9:00 p.m. and Saturday from 9:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. Pacific time.

How long do I have to send in my trade-in device if I am sending it back by mail?

Once you receive the trade-in kit, you have 14 days to prepare and send in your device. If you added a trade-in to an iPhone order, you may receive the kit along with your new device. Otherwise, it will be mailed to you separately as soon as your new product is ready for delivery or pickup.
